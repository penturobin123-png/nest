CLOUD SERVICE

Cloud services have fundamentally changed how individuals and businesses interact with technology. At its simplest, a cloud service refers to any resource—be it storage, processing power, or software—that is delivered over the internet rather than being hosted on a local computer or a physical server in your office. Instead of buying and maintaining expensive hardware, users "rent" these capabilities from providers like Google, Amazon, or Microsoft.
